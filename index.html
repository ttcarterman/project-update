In the same way as our modern societies, Mesopotamians belonged to different social classes.The kings of city-states, regions or empires were at the very top of the social ladder. They were regarded as the main connection with the local gods. The gods were said to have chosen their kings and, if they were happy with their leader, he and his kingdom would prosper.
